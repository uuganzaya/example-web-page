About us

Welcome to Off-Road Travel! We established our company to give you the BEST experience in the wilderness of our beautiful home land, western Mongolia. Whether you are wandering in the steppe or vanishing into the clouds in the mountain range our team will make these moments into a once in a lifetime experience.

Our professional team will accompany you in the maximum of comfort, we comprise of an experienced mountianeer, a chef and a mechanic, all are Japanese and English speakers.

Since we are all locals, we have carefully selected the most remote and beautiful routes possible, making sure that you will see and get to know our homeland in a way that not many can do. We will reach areas that are completely free of radio signals, where you will be able to fully reconnect with mother nature.
